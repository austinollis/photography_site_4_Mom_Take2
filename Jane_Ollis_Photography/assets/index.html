About Me

What do I do?

I take incredible pictures in multiple different settings. I love capturing moments in people's lives that they will be able to cherish forever. I have a family full of athletes, so I have been taking action shots during sporting events for over 25 years.

How do I do it?

I take my pictures with the Nimbus 2020, because it takes high quality photos every time. I have a lense that costs over $2000 so I can spot a flee sucking the blood out of a horses butt from a mile away.

Why hire me?

I will do my best to capture moments of your life that you would like to hold on to forever. I am very easy to work with, and taking photos for people is definitely one of my love languages so I put my heart and sould into every wedding, game or event I'm asked to shoot.
